Welcome to my travel blog around Poland!

In this travel blog, I am presenting four stunning places in Poland that I have had the pleasure of visiting. I hope that after reading my blog you would like to travel to these places. I am truly recommending you to do so!

Although the history of Poland is associated with numerous invasions and many years in captivity, many magnificent monuments and cities remained intact and are now tourist attractions for travellers from all over the world. All of them are enriched with beautiful history and landscape that we can admire in the territory of this country. Therefore, I invite you to find out more about the most incredible sites and cities worth seeing in Poland. Certainly, everyone will find something for themselves here. Feel free to share your favourites in the comments! At the end of this page, I allowed myself to display a map of all the locations that I will be talking about in my blogs.
